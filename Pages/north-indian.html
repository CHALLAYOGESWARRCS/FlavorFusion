North Indian Delights

Aloo Gobi

Ingredients: Potatoes, Cauliflower, Turmeric, Cumin

Preparation Steps:

  1. Sauté cumin seeds and spices in oil.
  2. Add chopped potatoes and cauliflower.
  3. Cook until tender, about 20 minutes.
  4. Serve with roti.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, Butter, Cream, Tomato, Spices

Preparation Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 2 hours.
  2. Cook chicken in butter until tender.
  3. Add tomato puree and cream, simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve with naan or rice.

Chole Bhature

Ingredients: Chickpeas, Flour, Spices, Oil

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak and cook chickpeas with spices.
  2. Knead flour into dough, roll into bhature.
  3. Fry bhature, serve with spiced chole.

Dal Makhani

Ingredients: Black Lentils, Kidney Beans, Cream, Butter

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak lentils and beans overnight, then boil.
  2. Cook with butter, cream, and spices for 1 hour.
  3. Garnish with cream.
  4. Serve with naan.

Malai Kofta

Ingredients: Paneer, Potato, Cream, Cashews

Preparation Steps:

  1. Mash paneer and potato, form into balls.
  2. Fry koftas, then simmer in creamy gravy.
  3. Garnish with cashews.
  4. Serve with rice.

Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, Yogurt, Tandoori Masala, Lemon

Preparation Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 4 hours.
  2. Grill or bake at 200°C for 30 minutes.
  3. Squeeze lemon juice on top.
  4. Serve with mint chutney.
