Korean Delights

Bulgogi

Ingredients: Beef, Soy Sauce, Sugar, Sesame Oil

Preparation Steps:

  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, sugar, and sesame oil for 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-fry until caramelized.
  3. Serve with lettuce wraps.

Galbi Jjim

Ingredients: Beef Short Ribs, Soy Sauce, Radish, Chestnuts

Preparation Steps:

  1. Marinate ribs in soy sauce and sugar.
  2. Simmer with radish and chestnuts for 1 hour.
  3. Cook until meat is tender.
  4. Serve with rice.

Haemul Pajeon

Ingredients: Flour, Seafood, Green Onion, Egg

Preparation Steps:

  1. Mix flour with water and egg for batter.
  2. Add seafood and green onions, spread on a pan.
  3. Cook until crispy on both sides.
  4. Serve with dipping sauce.

Kimbap

Ingredients: Nori, Rice, Carrot, Spinach, Tuna

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook seasoned rice and prepare fillings.
  2. Roll rice and fillings in nori sheets.
  3. Slice into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Serve with soy sauce.

Tteokbokki

Ingredients: Rice Cakes, Gochujang, Fish Cake, Sugar

Preparation Steps:

  1. Boil rice cakes in water until soft.
  2. Add gochujang and fish cake, simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in sugar to balance flavor.
  4. Serve hot.
