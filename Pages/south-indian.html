South Indian Delights

Appam

Ingredients: Rice, Coconut Milk, Yeast, Salt

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak rice, grind with coconut, add yeast, and ferment for 8 hours.
  2. Heat an appam pan, pour batter, and cook until edges are crispy.
  3. Flip and cook briefly, then remove.
  4. Serve with vegetable stew or curry.

Fish Curry

Ingredients: Fish, Coconut Milk, Tamarind, Spices

Preparation Steps:

  1. Marinate fish with turmeric and salt for 15 minutes.
  2. Sauté spices, add coconut milk and tamarind paste in a pan.
  3. Add fish, simmer for 15 minutes until cooked.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Idli

Ingredients: Rice, Urad Dal, Salt, Water

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 6 hours.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter and mix with salt.
  3. Ferment the batter for 8 hours in a warm place.
  4. Pour the batter into idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Kerala Parotta

Ingredients: All-Purpose Flour, Oil, Salt, Water

Preparation Steps:

  1. Knead flour with oil, salt, and water; rest the dough for 2 hours.
  2. Roll into thin layers, coil, and flatten into discs.
  3. Cook on a griddle with oil until golden and flaky.
  4. Serve with a spicy curry.

Upma

Ingredients: Semolina, Mustard Seeds, Curry Leaves, Vegetables

Preparation Steps:

  1. Roast semolina in a pan until aromatic, then set aside.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves, then sauté vegetables.
  3. Add water and semolina, stirring until fluffy and cooked.
  4. Serve hot with a side of chutney.

Uttapam

Ingredients: Rice, Urad Dal, Onion, Tomato, Green Chili

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak rice and urad dal, grind into a batter, and ferment overnight.
  2. Heat a pan, pour thick batter, and sprinkle chopped onion, tomato, and green chili.
  3. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
